## Idea
Crafting Connections is an AI-powered cultural tourism platform that bridges the gap between travelers and India’s authentic artisan heritage. Despite India being home to over 6.4 million artisans, 3,000+ traditional crafts, and 744 handicraft clusters, most tourists never discover these living cultural treasures because existing platforms require them to already know what to search for. At the same time, skilled artisans remain digitally invisible, dependent on middlemen, and disconnected from tourism opportunities.

Our platform introduces a location-aware Craft Discovery Engine that proactively recommends nearby artisan villages, workshops, and craft experiences based on a tourist’s location. Using a government-backed multi-tier verification system powered by Artisan Identity Cards, GI certifications, and community validation, the platform ensures authenticity and builds trust. Tourists can discover verified artisans, book workshops, explore curated craft trails, compare fair prices, and even purchase products with a “Ship Later” option, while artisans receive a multilingual digital storefront, booking management, and direct access to customers without excessive commissions.

Unlike existing travel apps, maps, or e-commerce platforms, Crafting Connections combines discovery, trust, and direct engagement into one ecosystem, transforming tourism from passive sightseeing into immersive cultural experiences.

The solution empowers artisans with sustainable livelihoods, preserves India’s traditional crafts by making them economically rewarding for future generations, strengthens cultural tourism, and creates a scalable Digital India platform that connects government-backed artisan infrastructure with millions of domestic and international travelers.
